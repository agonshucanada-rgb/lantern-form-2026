Lantern Side Applications

One Lantern Side consists of two names:

Benefactor Name — the person or entity offering memorial prayers (you, a friend, a family member, or a company/business).

Memorial Name — the deceased person, ancestor, family lineage, or spirit you wish to memorialize. A general description is acceptable if no specific name exists (e.g., "Victims of the Palestine-Israel War", "Aborted babies in Smith Family", "Spirits related to Kiplee Inc.").

If the Benefactor or Memorial Name is Japanese, it is recommended to write it in Japanese Kanji.
